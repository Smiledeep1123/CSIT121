The recipe is of PIZZA

The best pizza recipe ever created by John torode at goodfood website

ingredients: fresh yeast, sugar, flour, olive oil, mozzarella, basil, tomato sauce,vegetables

Steps

Mix the yeast and sugar with water and mix. Place the flour in mixer and mix it to make base. when the base is ready, apply tomato sauce to it. After that put cheese on it,and then add the toppings like vegatables you desire. then cook for 10-15 minutes depending on the temperature.
